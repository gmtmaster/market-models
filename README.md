# 📈 Market Models

A structured collection of independent quantitative research projects focused on **financial market behaviour, statistical modelling, and probability-based analysis**.

The objective of this repository is to explore repeatable market dynamics across different instruments through **data-driven research, regime detection, and intraday behavioural studies**.

---

## 🔍 Included Research Areas

### 📊 FX Session Behaviour Models

Research focused on **intraday FX structure**, session-based probability, and directional behaviour:

* Asia / Europe session directional bias
* Time-of-day price behaviour
* Intraday statistical observations

---

### 📈 Volatility Regime Classification

Regime-based market state analysis using rolling statistical features:

* volatility clustering
* high / low regime detection
* feature-based market classification

---

### 🧠 Ongoing Quantitative Experiments

Additional exploratory work in:

* supervised classification models
* feature engineering
* historical market pattern testing
